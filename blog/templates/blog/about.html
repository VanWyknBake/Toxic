About

Toxic Paradise's main goal is to report toxic players of the CODM South Africa community. If you encounter a toxic person online who just keeps popping up with a new name , you can report them here with their UID. We hope to provide a service for tournament hosts and clan leaders when accepting a new member into their clan. You can now search for a UID on Toxic Paradise to see if that person has ever been reported here for toxic / abusive behavior on the game , and make a decision accordingly. Help us cleans our community!



To report a player please create an account and log in.
