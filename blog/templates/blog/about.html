About

QSnippets (Quick Snippets) is a personal blog project to record code problems that I encounter while learning Django. Providing a quick, yet detailed snippet about the possible problem and the solution I used to fix it. For the time being the site will not be a publically launched site. Instead its just a reference tool for me to use while I master the art of Django!
-So basically im talking to myself here-

The majority of this website was built thanks to a very userfull Youtube tutorial series presented by Corey Shaefer. Although the course is a couple of years old, nothing in the lessons are outdated. I found it to be an incredibly informative tutorial!

To view or share snippets, please create an account and log in. You might just find the solution to your problem!
